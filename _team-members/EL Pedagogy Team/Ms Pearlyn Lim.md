<h4>Ms Pearlyn Lim Puay Leng</h4>
<p>Senior Academy Officer (Pedagogy)</p>
<p>Before joining AST, Pearlyn Lim gained substantial experience across media
and education. Her professional background includes serving for 11 years
as a Head of Department, following her valuable tenure as a Curriculum
Planning Officer at the Ministry of Education (MOE), and earlier work as
a journalist at Singapore Press Holdings.</p>
<p>Currently, Pearlyn is a doctoral candidate whose research interests include
the sensemaking of policies, supporting teacher professional development
and fostering collaborative practice for curriculum change. She is also
keen on strengthening assessment literacy and effective pedagogy for teaching
English Language and Literature.</p>
<p>In her free time, she enjoys quiet pursuits such as cooking and gardening,
as well as staying active through swimming and long walks in natural settings.</p>
