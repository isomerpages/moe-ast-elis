<h4>Ms Sabina Binte Jumari</h4>
<p>Academy Officer (Pedagogy)</p>
<p>Prior to joining AST, Sabina has been with a secondary school for about
6 years, where she taught English and Social Studies, and coordinated the
upper secondary curriculum.</p>
<p>Sabina believes in making learning intentional, relevant, and connected
to real-world contexts. She is passionate about building students’ engagement
and confidence through meaningful hands-on activities. With a growth-oriented
mindset, she looks forward to collaborating with fellow educators to explore
innovative teaching approaches that inspire curiosity and critical thinking.</p>
<p>In her leisure time, she enjoys practising yoga and pilates, as well as
travelling to experience new cultures and uncovering fresh perspectives
to bring back to the classroom.</p>
