<h4>Ms Anna Mathew</h4>
<p><strong>Deputy Director/ Pedagogy</strong>
</p>
<p>Anna brings over 15 years of educational experience to her role at AST.
Prior to joining ELIS, she served as the Vice-Principal of a primary school
for five years. Before that, she headed the English department at a secondary
school and worked as a Curriculum Planning Officer at MOE’s English Language
and Literature Branch.</p>
<p>Anna believes in empowering teachers to continually refine their craft
and, more importantly, to find joy in the process.</p>
<p>Outside of her work, Anna enjoys reading, travelling and experimenting
with new recipes in the kitchen.</p>
