<p>The ELIS Classroom Inquiry Special Interest Group (SIG) held their Ideation
Session in Zoom, on Thursday, 29 February 2024 (2.30 – 4.30 pm). It was
attended by 13 members comprising EL teachers and teacher leaders from
eight primary and secondary schools.</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="CI SIG Ideation Session 2024" src="/images/Learning Communities/CI_SIG_Support_Ideation_Session.png">
</div>
<p>At the Ideation Sesion, the members took turns to talk about their areas
of inquiry and asked for feedback on their inquiry questions and plans.
Some of the areas of inquiry discussed included the following:</p>
<p>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Effect of freewriting on students’
motivation towards writing</p>
<p>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Supporting reluctant readers</p>
<p>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Technology-enabled tools and metacognitive
thinking</p>
<p>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Use of LangFA to develop students'
metacognition through feedback</p>
<p>Following the Ideation Session, members were assigned to ELIS Specialists
to support them in drawing their inquiry plans. Learn more about the
<a href="https://elis.moe.edu.sg/learning-communities/classroom-inquiry-sig/sig-support/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">Calendar of Support</a>for the ELIS Classroom Inquiry SIG.</p>
