# Artful Strokes 

Murals dance  
in gay-light sparkles  
Telling stories, of Marvels!  
The bright skyline aligns  
Buildings tall, oh SO fine!  
  
I spy  
I see  
Of popcorns! Rain clouds!  
Fists-fours! Teardrops! High-fours!  
Artworks of pastel, a GALORE  
   
Mustard glow  
Such floral fun  
Playful artistry  
in the sun  
A wonder!  
I walk on  
with heart’s desires  
Stark as light  
Art stokes…  
Differences evoke
