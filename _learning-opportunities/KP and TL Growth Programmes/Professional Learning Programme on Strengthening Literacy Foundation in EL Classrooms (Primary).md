<p><em>How do I address the diverse needs of learners in my EL classroom? How do I strengthen literacy foundation in students who have language learning difficulties? What choices of learning experiences, teaching approaches, assessment modes, texts and EdTech tools can I make to address the varied learning needs of my students?</em>
</p>
<h3>Overview</h3>
<p>The objectives of this PLP are to:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>raise awareness of KP, TLs &amp; teachers of EL on addressing the diverse
needs of learners in the teaching, learning &amp; assessment of EL;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>build knowledge of effective practices to strengthen literacy foundation
in the teaching, learning &amp; assessment of EL; and</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>exchange ideas, collaborate and network in a&nbsp;Networked Learning Community
to&nbsp;strengthen literacy foundation in the teaching, learning &amp;
assessment of EL.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<h3>Learning Goals</h3>
<p>By the end of this learning programme, you will be able to build and apply
the following in the teaching, learning &amp; assessment of EL:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Knowledge of Learners’ Needs</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Knowledge of:
<br>− Subject Content and &nbsp;
<br>− Pedagogy including knowledge of&nbsp;effective practices to strengthen
literacy foundation for the realisation of Pedagogical Content Knowledge</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Knowledge of ELS 2020</p>
</li>
</ul>
