[ELIS e-Digest Issue 3](/files/making-writing-and-representing-meaningful.pdf)

In this issue, ‘Making Writing and Representing Meaningful', you can learn about creating meaningful writing and representing experiences for students and how to engage them as writers.   
  
Explore the classroom practices and resources featured in this issue, which aim at helping students develop learner strategies for writing and representing.

[ELIS e-Digest Issue 2](/files/'learning-about-our-learners'-elis-e-digest-jan-22.pdf)
		 
In this issue, ‘Learning about our Learners’, you will learn about what it means to place learners at the heart of the teaching, learning and assessment process.   
  
Explore the classroom practices and resources featured in this issue to enable you to respond to variations in your students’ readiness, interests and learning profiles.

[ELIS e-Digest Inaugural Issue ](/files/'let's-talk-about-talk'-elis-e-digest-july-2021.pdf)

In this issue, ‘Let’s Talk about Talk’, you will learn about the what, why and how of classroom talk.    
  
You can listen to the views of Mr Wong Siew Hoong, Director-General of Education, on the value of talk. You can also find out about the classroom practices which encourage students to participate in exploratory talk.
